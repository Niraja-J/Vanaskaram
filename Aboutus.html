About Vanaskaram

At Vanaskaram, we believe every spice carries the story of the land it comes from and the people who cultivate it. Our journey began with five friends and co-founders who shared a common passion for travelling across India's diverse landscapes, exploring regional cuisines, and experiencing the rich traditions that make every state unique. From the bustling spice markets of Kochi to the aromatic plantations of the Western Ghats and the vibrant chilli fields of Karnataka, every journey introduced us to flavours that deserved to be celebrated. During our travels, we discovered that many authentic regional spices and handcrafted condiments were produced by small farming communities using traditional methods passed down through generations. Yet, these remarkable products often remained confined to local markets, making it difficult for people across the country to experience their true quality and heritage. Inspired by these encounters, we founded Vanaskaram with a simple vision—to bridge the gap between dedicated farmers and modern kitchens. We work closely with trusted growers and regional producers to source spices responsibly while preserving their freshness, aroma, and authenticity. Every product is carefully selected, hygienically packed, and delivered with the same care and respect with which it was cultivated. Today, Vanaskaram is more than just a spice brand. It is a celebration of India's culinary diversity, the dedication of its farming communities, and the timeless recipes that bring families together around a meal. Whether it's the bold Malabar black pepper of Kerala, the vibrant turmeric of Telangana, the fiery Byadgi chillies of Karnataka, or the fragrant cardamom of the Western Ghats, every ingredient we offer reflects our commitment to quality, sustainability, and authenticity. As five co-founders, our journey continues with every region we explore, every farmer we meet, and every customer who chooses Vanaskaram. We hope that every meal prepared with our products carries not only exceptional flavour but also the story of the people and places that make India's culinary heritage truly extraordinary.

Our Mission

To preserve India's rich culinary heritage by connecting regional farmers with modern kitchens through authentic, responsibly sourced spices and handcrafted condiments.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted regional spice brand by celebrating authenticity, supporting farming communities, and bringing the true flavours of every region to homes across the country.
