Discover the Flavours of India, One Recipe at a Time

Every spice has a story, and every recipe is an opportunity to experience it. Our collection of regional recipes celebrates India's rich culinary heritage by showcasing authentic dishes prepared with carefully sourced spices and handcrafted condiments from Vanaskaram. From comforting family favourites to traditional regional delicacies, each recipe is designed to inspire home cooks while bringing the true flavours of India to every kitchen.

Authentic Recipes

Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani

Experience the royal heritage of Hyderabad. This slow-cooked, dum-style biryani perfectly layers aromatic basmati rice with heavily spiced, tender marinated chicken. Bring out the authentic Nizami flavour using our premium whole spices like shahi jeera, mace, and green cardamom.

Paneer Butter Masala

A rich, creamy, and mildly sweet North Indian classic. Soft paneer cubes are simmered in a silky tomato-cashew gravy loaded with butter and cream. Finish this restaurant-style luxury at home with a pinch of our fragrant Kasuri Methi and signature Garam Masala.

Jeera Rice

Simple, elegant, and the ultimate companion to any rich curry. Fluffy, perfectly separated grains of long rice are tossed in pure ghee and tempered with the earthy warmth of cumin seeds. Elevate this staple using bold, aromatic Vanaskaram Cumin Seeds.

Chicken Curry

A robust, homestyle Indian chicken curry featuring a deeply matured flavour profile. Cooked with an onion-tomato base and packed with a kick of robust ground chillies, it is best enjoyed with hot rotis or rice. Maximize the deep, rustic flavour with our pure Coriander and Turmeric Powders.

Dal Tadka

Comfort food at its absolute finest. Smooth yellow lentils are cooked to perfection and finished with an assertive, sizzling dhaba-style tempering of mustard seeds, garlic, hing, and dried red chillies. The ideal canvas for our high-pungency spices.

Vegetable Pulao

A quick, colourful, one-pot meal packed with fresh seasonal vegetables and seasoned with subtle whole spices. Learn the secret to making perfectly fluffy rice and elevate every bite with our aromatic bay leaves and cloves.
